# Design Rules — McKinsey Consulting Style (v1)

## Background & Layout
- White background (FFFFFF) for content slides
- 16:9 layout (10" x 5.625")
- Left/right margins: 0.5"

## Color Palette
- Text: black (000000) or near-black (333333)
- Table highlight columns: light blue (D6EAF8) or light teal (D5F5E3)
- Row labels: bold black on white
- Footer/footnotes: grey (666666)
- NO bright, saturated, or dark background colors

## Typography
- Title: Georgia Bold, 22-26pt, black
- Subtitle: Calibri, 12pt, grey (#666666)
- Table headers: Calibri Bold, 11pt, white on navy header row
- Table body: Calibri, 10pt, black
- Footer: Calibri, 8pt, grey

## Title
- Insight-driven "so-what" conclusion as title
- Example: "Blue and Green hydrogen are two low-carbon production methods of H2 with the highest future potential"
- NOT a topic label

## Table/Data
- Structured comparison table
- Bold row labels on left
- Column headers centered
- Light color fill on highlighted columns (Blue H2 = light blue, Green H2 = light green)
- Thin grey borders (0.5pt, color E0E0E0)

## Structural Elements
- Thin divider line below title: 1pt, dark grey (333333), full width
- Footer: "McKinsey & Company" + page number, right-aligned, y=5.3
- Footnotes: 8pt grey text above footer, y=5.0
- Source line with superscript numbers

## Layout Positions
- Title: x=0.5, y=0.2, w=9
- Subtitle: x=0.5, y=0.9
- Divider line: x=0.5, y=1.2, w=9
- Table: x=0.5, y=1.4, fills to y~4.8
- Footnotes: x=0.5, y=5.0
- Footer: x=7.5, y=5.3
