# Footer & Structural Elements — Mandatory on Every Slide

## Slide Structure (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Title Area (Top)
- Position: top-left, within margins
- Insight-driven title in Georgia, 22–28pt, bold, navy (#003A70)
- Left-aligned

### 2. Thin Divider Line
- **MANDATORY** — never omit this
- Position: directly below the title, with ~0.15" gap
- Style: solid line, 0.75–1pt weight
- Color: light grey `#CCCCCC` or `#999999`
- Width: spans from left margin to right margin (~9" on a standard 10" slide)

### 3. Body Content Area
- The main content: tables, charts, text, diagrams
- Clean, minimal styling (see DESIGN_RULES.md)
- Use thin borders, no heavy fills, white background

### 4. Source Attribution
- Position: bottom of the content area, above the footer line
- Format: `Source: [description]; McKinsey analysis`
- Font: Calibri/Arial, 8–9pt, regular
- Color: medium grey `#666666`
- Alignment: LEFT-aligned (never centered)

### 5. Footer Divider Line
- **MANDATORY** — never omit this
- Position: above the footer, separating content from footer
- Style: solid line, 0.5–0.75pt weight
- Color: light grey `#CCCCCC`
- Width: full slide width (margin to margin)

### 6. Footer Area (Bottom)
- **MANDATORY** — never omit this
- Left side: `CONFIDENTIAL` or company/project identifier in 8pt grey
- Right side: Page number (e.g., `1`) in 8pt grey
- Font: Calibri/Arial, 8pt
- Color: `#666666`
- The footer sits within the bottom margin area

## Spacing Guidelines
- Title top to slide top: ~0.5"
- Title bottom to divider line: ~0.1–0.15"
- Divider line to body content: ~0.2–0.3"
- Body content bottom to source text: ~0.15"
- Source text to footer line: ~0.1"
- Footer line to footer text: ~0.05"
- Footer text to slide bottom: ~0.2"

## Checklist Before Finalizing Any Slide
- [ ] Background is pure white (#FFFFFF)
- [ ] Title is an insight-driven statement (not a topic label)
- [ ] Thin divider line present below the title
- [ ] Body content uses only navy/grey/white palette
- [ ] No thick borders or solid colored fills
- [ ] Source text is left-aligned in grey
- [ ] Footer divider line present
- [ ] Footer has confidentiality text (left) and page number (right)
- [ ] All text has good contrast against white background
- [ ] Overall impression is clean, minimal, and professional
