# Design Rules — McKinsey-Style Management Consulting Slides

## CRITICAL: Overall Aesthetic
These slides must look like they came from a top-tier management consulting firm
(McKinsey, BCG, Bain). They must be **rigorous, restrained, analytical, and professional**.
They must NEVER look like infographics, marketing materials, or generic templates.

---

## Background
- **ALWAYS use a WHITE background** (`FFFFFF`).
- NEVER use dark backgrounds, gradients, or colored backgrounds.
- The slide canvas must be clean white at all times.

## Color Palette — Restrained & Professional
Use ONLY this McKinsey-standard palette. No other colors are permitted:

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary text | Dark Navy / Black | `192A3E` or `000000` |
| Secondary text / labels | Medium Grey | `5A6A7A` |
| Accent / highlights | McKinsey Blue | `004B87` |
| Light accent / table fills | Light Blue-Grey | `D6E4F0` |
| Divider lines | Medium Grey | `8C9DAD` |
| Table header background | Dark Navy | `192A3E` |
| Table header text | White | `FFFFFF` |

**FORBIDDEN colors:** Teal, neon, seafoam, mint, bright green, bright yellow, orange, red (unless for specific data callouts). No vibrant or saturated colors.

## Typography
- **Title:** Georgia or similar serif font, 24–30pt, bold, color `192A3E` (dark navy)
- **Subtitle / section headers:** Calibri or Arial, 14–16pt, bold, color `192A3E`
- **Body text:** Calibri or Arial, 11–14pt, regular, color `333333` or `000000`
- **Footnotes / sources:** Calibri, 8–9pt, color `5A6A7A` (medium grey)
- **Footer text:** Calibri, 8–9pt, color `5A6A7A`

## Slide Title — MUST Be Insight-Driven
- The title is the MOST important element. It must be a **"so-what" conclusion**, NOT a topic label.
- **BAD example:** "Dutch Hydrogen Strategy" (this is just a topic label)
- **GOOD example:** "The Netherlands must invest €15B+ in hydrogen infrastructure by 2030 to meet its decarbonization targets"
- **GOOD example:** "Hydrogen production costs are projected to fall 60% by 2030, making green hydrogen cost-competitive with grey"
- The title should communicate the key takeaway — what the reader should conclude from the slide.
- Place title at the TOP of the slide, left-aligned, spanning most of the slide width.

## Horizontal Divider Line Under Title — MANDATORY
- **ALWAYS draw a thin horizontal line** directly below the title text.
- Line color: `8C9DAD` (medium grey) or `004B87` (McKinsey blue)
- Line weight: 0.75–1.0pt
- Line spans from left margin to right margin
- Positioned ~0.15" below the title text baseline
- This divider is a NON-NEGOTIABLE structural element.

## Data Presentation — Use Structured Tables
- When presenting data, metrics, or comparisons, use a **structured data table** — NOT timelines, process flows, or infographic icons.
- Tables should have:
  - A dark navy header row (`192A3E` background, `FFFFFF` text)
  - Alternating row fills: white and light blue-grey (`D6E4F0`)
  - Thin borders in `8C9DAD`
  - Left-aligned text, right-aligned numbers
  - Clear column headers with units
- If a chart is used instead of a table, it must be clean and minimal (no 3D, no heavy gridlines, no decorative elements).

## Footer — MANDATORY
Every slide MUST have a footer bar at the very bottom containing:
- **Left side:** Company/project name (e.g., "McKinsey & Company" or client context)
- **Right side:** Page number
- **Optional center:** Confidentiality notice ("Confidential — for internal use only")
- Footer text: Calibri 8pt, color `5A6A7A`
- A thin horizontal line (`8C9DAD`, 0.5pt) should separate the footer from the slide body, positioned ~0.4" from the bottom of the slide.

## Source Line
- If the slide references external data, include a **source line** just above the footer.
- Format: "Source: [source name], [year]"
- Font: Calibri, 8–9pt, color `5A6A7A` (medium grey)
- Must have HIGH contrast against the white background — NEVER place light text on a dark background.

## Layout & Spacing
- Margins: 0.5" on all sides (left, right, top, bottom)
- Title area: top 1.0–1.3" of slide
- Content area: below divider line, above footer
- 0.3–0.5" spacing between content blocks
- Left-align all text (no center-alignment except for table cell data)
- Keep the layout clean and structured — generous white space is preferred over cramming

## What to AVOID
- ❌ Dark or colored backgrounds
- ❌ Neon, teal, mint, or vibrant color schemes
- ❌ Infographic-style icons, illustrations, or decorative elements
- ❌ Timeline/process-flow layouts when a table would be clearer
- ❌ Generic topic-label titles (always use insight-driven "so-what" titles)
- ❌ Missing divider line under the title
- ❌ Missing footer with company name and page number
- ❌ Low-contrast text (especially source/footnote text)
- ❌ Center-aligned body text
- ❌ Decorative fonts or unusual type treatments
